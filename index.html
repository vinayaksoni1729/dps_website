About Us

Welcome to Delhi Public School, where education meets excellence. Our school has been dedicated to providing quality education for over 50 years.

Our mission is to nurture the intellectual, physical, and emotional growth of our students, preparing them to be responsible citizens and lifelong learners.

At DPS, we offer a comprehensive curriculum, a caring and supportive faculty, and a range of extracurricular activities to enrich the educational experience of our students.

We are committed to creating a safe and inclusive learning environment where every student can thrive. Come join us on this journey of knowledge and discovery!
